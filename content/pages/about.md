# About

I am a Data Scientist at Ryax Technologies, working on AI workflow orchestration for a low-code platform that deploys analytics and AI workflows across hybrid Cloud-HPC infrastructures.

My current work connects production Python, Kubernetes, Slurm, workflow automation, LLM agents, RAG pipelines, and multi-objective scheduling. The goal is practical automation: placing workloads across heterogeneous infrastructure while balancing cost, performance, privacy, and operational constraints.

Before Ryax, I spent five years at Bull/Atos on HPC data management and performance modeling: recommendation systems for data placement, burst-buffer sizing, workflow optimization, and multivariate time-series analysis for scientific and system signals.

## Focus Areas
